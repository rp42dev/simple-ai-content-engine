## What Are Professional Teeth Whitening Treatments?

Professional teeth whitening uses stronger bleaching agents like carbamide peroxide or hydrogen peroxide, applied under dentist supervision to lighten the natural colour of your teeth. These treatments are carefully tailored to your teeth and gum health to make sure the process is both effective and safe.

Unlike store-bought gels or strips, professional whitening is customised: the bleaching gel strength, application method, and treatment length are adjusted based on your needs. This approach also reduces the chances of gum irritation or tooth sensitivity compared to unsupervised products.

The whitening works by penetrating the enamel to break down stains caused by foods, drinks, smoking, and aging, giving you a fresher, brighter smile.

## Overview of Types of Professional Teeth Whitening Treatments

There are two main types of professional whitening you’ll find at dental clinics in Celbridge:

- **In-office Whitening Procedures**: Done in the clinic by the dentist, these offer fast, dramatic results in a single or couple of sessions.
- **Take-home Whitening Kits Provided by Dentists**: Custom trays and bleaching gel you use at home over a few weeks, under dentist guidance.

Many clinics combine these methods to give you the best result — starting with an in-office session for quick impact, then using take-home kits to maintain or gradually continue whitening.

## In-Office Whitening Procedures in Celbridge

### What to Expect in an In-Clinic Whitening Session

When you come to your appointment, the dentist will first protect your gums with a special barrier to keep the bleaching gel away from soft tissues. Then, a strong whitening gel is applied directly to your teeth.

Many clinics, including ours at From To, may use a special light or laser to activate the gel, speeding up the whitening process. The session usually lasts one to two hours.

Typically, one to two sessions are enough to reach your desired shade, especially for deeper, stubborn stains.

### Benefits of In-Office Whitening

- **Quick results**: You’ll notice a visibly whiter smile straight away.
- **Safe and monitored**: A dental professional supervises the entire process, adjusting gel strength to match your tooth sensitivity.
- **Customized treatment**: The dentist can tailor the procedure based on your specific staining and oral health.

### Considerations for Patients in Celbridge

Some patients experience mild tooth sensitivity during or after treatment. This usually fades within a few days. If you’re preparing for a special occasion or want a significant change quickly, this is an excellent option.

Keep in mind that in-office treatments generally cost more—typically between €300 and €500 per session—but you get the advantage of clinical expertise and immediate results.

## Take-Home Whitening Kits Provided by Dentists

### How Professional Take-Home Kits Work

If you prefer whitening at your own pace, a custom take-home kit might be ideal. After a visit to the clinic where we take impressions of your teeth, you’ll receive trays made to fit perfectly.

You’ll apply a milder bleaching gel inside the trays and wear them as instructed, usually for about an hour a day over one to two weeks. Follow-up appointments help us monitor your progress and adjust if necessary.

### Benefits of Take-Home Kits

- **Convenience**: You can whiten your teeth at home around your schedule.
- **Gentler process**: Because the gel is lower strength, the risk of sensitivity is reduced.
- **Cost-effective**: These kits usually cost between €150 and €300 and still come with dentist monitoring.
- **Good for maintenance**: Perfect for keeping your smile bright after an in-office treatment.

### Who Should Consider Take-Home Kits?

Take-home whitening works well for mild to moderate staining or for patients who want a subtler, gradual whitening effect. It’s a great choice if you have sensitive teeth or prefer to space out the treatment.
